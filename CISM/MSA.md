# CISM Third-Party Management: Master Service Agreement (MSA)

## 1. Governance Objective
The MSA provides a repeatable legal framework to ensure third-party vendors adhere to the organization's risk appetite and security standards.

## 2. Critical Security Provisions
* **Security Standards:** Requirement to maintain specific certifications (ISO 27001, SOC 2 Type II).
* **Data Handling:** Instructions for data encryption, storage locations (geographical constraints), and disposal.
* **Sub-Contracting:** Requirement for the provider to disclose if they use "fourth-party" vendors to handle your data.
* **Insurance:** Requirement for the vendor to maintain Cyber Liability Insurance.

## 3. The "Exit Strategy"
Every MSA must include a "Return of Assets" clause. This ensures that when the contract ends:
1. All data is returned in a readable format.
2. All provider copies are securely wiped/sanitized.
3. Access rights (PAM/IAM) are immediately revoked.

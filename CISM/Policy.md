# CISM Governance: Policy Framework

## 1. Core Definitions
* **Policy:** Mandatory, high-level statement of management's intent. (The "What" and "Why").
* **Standard:** Mandatory, specific requirements to ensure consistency. (The "Must Use").
* **Procedure:** Mandatory, step-by-step instructions. (The "How-To").
* **Guideline:** Non-mandatory, best-practice recommendations. (The "Should").

## 2. Policy Lifecycle
1. **Development:** Alignment with business objectives and risk appetite.
2. **Approval:** Signed off by the appropriate level of management.
3. **Publication:** Disseminated to all relevant stakeholders.
4. **Awareness:** Ensuring users understand and agree to the policy.
5. **Review:** Periodic updates (usually annually) or after significant changes.

## 3. Compliance & Enforcement
A policy is only effective if it includes:
* **Scope:** Who and what does this apply to?
* **Compliance:** How will we measure adherence?
* **Exceptions:** The formal process for requesting a waiver.
* **Sanctions:** The consequences for non-compliance.

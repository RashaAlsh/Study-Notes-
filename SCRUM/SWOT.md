# CISM Strategic Planning: SWOT Analysis

## Internal Factors
### Strengths (S)
* **Governance Alignment:** Strong relationship between CISO and the Board.
* **Resilience:** Mature Disaster Recovery (DR) and Business Continuity (BCP) plans.
* **Architecture:** Well-defined Zero Trust architecture.

### Weaknesses (W)
* **Resource Gaps:** Lack of specialized cloud security expertise.
* **Technical Debt:** Unpatched legacy systems supporting critical processes.
* **Culture:** Low compliance with security policies in remote branches.

## External Factors
### Opportunities (O)
* **Automation:** Leveraging AI for faster incident triage and response.
* **Compliance:** Using GDPR/CCPA requirements to justify budget for data privacy.
* **Outsourcing:** Partnering with an MSSP to provide 24/7 monitoring.

### Threats (T)
* **Threat Landscape:** Emerging sophisticated phishing and social engineering.
* **Regulatory Pressure:** New, stricter fines for data breaches.
* **Supply Chain:** Vulnerabilities in third-party software vendors.
